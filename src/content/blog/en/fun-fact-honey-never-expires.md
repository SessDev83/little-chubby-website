## Honey Never Goes Bad! 🍯

This is one of the coolest food facts: **honey literally never expires!**

Scientists found pots of honey in ancient Egyptian tombs that were over **3,000 years old** — and it was still perfectly good to eat! Honey's natural chemistry (low moisture, high acidity, and natural hydrogen peroxide) makes it impossible for bacteria to grow.

### Sweet Science Time 🎨

Next time you enjoy honey with your family, share this fact! And when coloring food-themed pages, ask your little ones: *"What would you store in a time capsule that lasts forever?"*

**Bonus fact:** Bees have to visit about 2 million flowers to make just one pound of honey. That's a lot of buzzing! 🐝
