## An Octopus Has Three Hearts! 🐙

Here's one that will blow your kids' minds: **an octopus has THREE hearts!**

Two hearts pump blood to its gills (where it breathes underwater), and one heart pumps blood to the rest of its body. And here's the extra-cool part: **octopus blood is blue!** That's because it uses copper instead of iron to carry oxygen — which works better in cold, deep ocean water.

### Color & Learn 🎨

When coloring an octopus, try making each heart a different color. Ask your kids: *"If you had three hearts, what would each one be for?"*

Maybe one heart for love, one for bravery, and one for silliness? 💙
