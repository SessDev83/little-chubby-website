## Hidden Colors in Nature! 🐝

Humans cannot see ultraviolet light, but some animals can. Bees use ultraviolet patterns on flowers to help find nectar.

### Color & Discover 🎨

Color a flower twice: once for people and once for bees. Ask: What secret pattern would your flower show?
