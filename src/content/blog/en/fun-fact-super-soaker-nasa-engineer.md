## A Space Engineer Invented the Super Soaker! 🚀

The Super Soaker feels like pure backyard fun, but its story has a science twist. **It was invented by Lonnie Johnson, an engineer who worked on NASA space projects.**

Johnson trained as an engineer and worked on serious technology, including projects connected to space missions. While experimenting at home, he noticed a powerful spray of water and began developing a high-performance water toy.

That idea became the Super Soaker, one of the most famous water toys in the world. It is a great reminder that inventions can start with curiosity, tinkering, and one surprising splash.
