## Butterflies Taste With Their Feet! 🦋

Here's a fun fact that will amaze your little ones: **butterflies don't taste food with their mouths — they taste it with their feet!**

When a butterfly lands on a leaf or flower, tiny sensors on the bottom of their feet instantly tell them whether it's something tasty or not. This helps mama butterflies find the perfect leaves to lay their eggs on, so their caterpillar babies will have the right food to eat when they hatch.

### Try This at Home! 🎨

Next time you're coloring a butterfly, talk about this fun fact with your kids. Ask them: *"What if YOU could taste food just by stepping on it?"* — you'll be surprised by the hilarious answers!

**Fun challenge:** Draw a butterfly landing on your favorite food. What would it taste?
