## Tiny Birds With Big Flying Skills! 🐦

Hummingbirds can hover in place and fly backward because their wings move in a figure-eight pattern. That helps them sip nectar from flowers.

### Color & Discover 🎨

Draw a hummingbird hovering beside bright flowers. Ask: If you could fly backward, where would you zoom first?
