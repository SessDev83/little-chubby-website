## Neutron Stars Are Super Dense! ⭐

When a giant star explodes, its core can collapse into a neutron star. The matter gets packed so tightly that one teaspoon could weigh as much as all humans on Earth combined.

### Color & Discover 🎨

Draw a tiny teaspoon next to a giant space scale. Ask your kids: "How can something so small be so heavy?"
