## Can a Frog Jump Higher Than a House? 🐸

**Of course! Houses can't JUMP!** 🏠😂

The frog tried to teach the house once, but it just sat there. *"You're so GROUNDED,"* said the frog. 🤣

---

**Silly question joke!** Ask your kids this one and watch their little brains work it out.

**Fun activity:** Draw a frog leaping over a tiny house — color the frog bright green and give the house a surprised face! 🎨🐸
