## Your Eyes Are Color Experts! 🌈

Human eyes can tell apart roughly 10 million color shades. That is why sunsets and rainbows can look so rich and detailed.

### Color & Discover 🎨

Make a mini gradient from light to dark. Ask: "Which two colors look almost the same to you?"
