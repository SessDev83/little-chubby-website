## Why Did the Chicken Cross the Playground? 🐔

**To get to the other SLIDE!** 🛝😂

And when she got there, the duck was already waiting at the bottom saying, *"What took you so CLUCKING long?"* 🦆

---

**Playground joke!** Tell this one at the park and watch the kids crack up on the swings.

**Fun activity:** Draw a chicken going down a rainbow-colored slide — don't forget the feathers flying everywhere! 🎨🌈
