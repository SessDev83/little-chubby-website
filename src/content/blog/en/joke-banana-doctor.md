## Why Did the Banana Go to the Doctor? 🍌

**Because it wasn't PEELING well!** 🤒😂

The doctor said, *"Don't worry — you just need to SPLIT and get some rest!"*

And the banana replied, *"But doc, I'm already going bananas!"* 🤣

---

**Breakfast joke!** Tell this one while peeling a banana in the morning — instant giggles.

**Fun activity:** Draw a banana wearing a tiny doctor's coat and stethoscope. Color the coat white and the banana the brightest yellow you can find! 🎨
