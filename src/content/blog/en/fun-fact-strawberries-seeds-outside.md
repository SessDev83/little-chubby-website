## Seeds on the Outside! 🍓

The little dots on a strawberry are tiny fruits called achenes, and each one has a seed inside. That makes strawberries extra unusual.

### Color & Discover 🎨

Draw a giant strawberry and count the tiny dots. Ask: What pattern would you give a pretend fruit?
