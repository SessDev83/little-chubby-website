## The Moon Moves the Sea! 🌙

The Moon's gravity pulls on Earth's water. As Earth turns, ocean places can experience high tide and low tide.

### Color & Discover 🎨

Draw the Moon tugging gently on ocean waves. Ask: What would you collect on a beach at low tide?
