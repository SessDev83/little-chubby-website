## What Do You Call a Bear With No Teeth? 🐻

**A GUMMY bear!** 🍬😂

He tried to eat honey, but it just kept sliding out. Now he only eats gummy worms — it feels like family! 🐛

---

**Snack time joke!** Tell this one next time you're sharing gummy bears with your kiddos.

**Fun activity:** Draw the friendliest, toothless bear you can imagine and color him in candy colors — pink, green, yellow, and orange! 🎨
