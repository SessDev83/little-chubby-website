## Orange Fruit, Green Peel! 🍊

In warm tropical climates, ripe oranges can keep a green peel because temperatures do not drop enough to remove chlorophyll.

Draw two oranges: one orange peel and one green peel. Ask: "How can we know fruit is ripe besides color?"
